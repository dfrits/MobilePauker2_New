This App was developed as a part of my Master Thesis in 2018 and then redesigned at my study abroad. Its purpose is to enable easy cross-platform-learning with flash cards.

Pauker is a text based flash card application. It uses a combination of ultra-short term, short term, and long term memory.
Mobile Pauker++ is an Android version of Pauker and compatible with version 1.7 and above.

Usage

It is very easy to use. You have the choice to

  • add immediately cards and then save the Lesson,
  • associate the App with your Dropbox account. Dropbox creates a folder at your account at "Apps" called "Mobile Pauker++". Then just save your lesson on your PC in this folder and synchronise them or
  • download a lesson from the official website of Pauker. (It's not neccesary to look for the App folder. Just open the lesson in the browser.)

Explained shortly

USTM = Ultra Shortterm Memory STM = Shortterm Memory

For further Information have a look at the documentation website of the Pauker. There you can find a detailed user guide.
